# ANALYSIS: Kapitola 9 - Postoj Víťaza (Winner's Attitude)

**Chapter Summary:** This chapter reveals the core operating system of people who succeed at everything. It's not technique or luck—it's a consistent assumption that life supports them, every situation works in their favor, and they deserve the best. The chapter teaches "segment intending" (deciding outcomes before they manifest) and demonstrates that perspective determines reality more than circumstances do.
